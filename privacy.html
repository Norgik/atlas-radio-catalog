Bølge Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026

Bølge does not collect, store, or transmit personal data. All processing happens on your device.

What We Don't Collect

  • No accounts, no registration
  • No personal information
  • No location tracking (weather uses approximate city)
  • No analytics
  • No advertising SDKs

On-Device Processing

AI transcription uses Apple's on-device Speech Recognition. Audio is processed locally, never sent to servers.

Third-Party

Bølge connects to public radio streams and RSS feeds directly. Weather via Open-Meteo (open-meteo.com).
